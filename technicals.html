Technical Analysis

Master price action, chart patterns and momentum indicators to find optimal entry and exit points.

Core Tools

  • Trends: Spot higher highs and higher lows or ranges to understand market structure.
  • Support & Resistance: Identify key price levels where buyers or sellers step in.
  • Momentum Indicators: Use RSI, MACD and moving averages to gauge strength and timing.
  • Volume: Confirm price moves with increasing or decreasing volume for validity.

Putting It Into Practice

Combine multiple indicators to build a trading plan. For example, look for breakouts above resistance with rising volume and a bullish MACD crossover, or spot reversal signals when RSI diverges from price at support. Our AI highlights these patterns, saving you time.
